# Simply Soil Services

## Service Offerings

### 1. Customized Lawn Care Programs
Our seven-step lawn care program is a full season of personalized care for the needs of your property.

**Key Features:**
- Full season coverage
- Personalized to your property's specific needs
- Seven comprehensive steps

---

### 2. Tree and Shrub Care Program
One of our most underrated services by far. This program is a once a month, seven-step application. It's designed to maximize blooms, color and overall health while protecting your trees and shrubs from the elements.

**Key Features:**
- Monthly applications
- Seven-step comprehensive program
- Maximizes blooms and color
- Protects from environmental elements
- Improves overall plant health

---

### 3. Mosquito and Tick Control
An important program designed to minimize mosquito and tick activity. This increases comfort and decreases the potential spread of disease on your property.

**Key Features:**
- Reduces mosquito and tick populations
- Increases outdoor comfort
- Disease prevention focus
- Family and pet safety

---

### 4. Core Aeration and Overseed (Spring and Fall)
The single most beneficial thing you can do for your lawn. Aeration opens up draining, loosens soil and gives your lawn's root system a chance to spread and strengthen.

**Key Features:**
- Available in Spring and Fall
- Improves soil drainage
- Loosens compacted soil
- Strengthens root systems
- Promotes lawn density through overseeding

---

### 5. Lawn Mowing
Weekly lawn mowing services that include edging and blowing.

**Key Features:**
- Weekly service
- Professional edging
- Complete cleanup with blowing
- Consistent lawn appearance

---

### 6. Mulch Bed Applications
We remove any existing weeds from mulch beds by pulling from the root followed by applying a preventative twice per year. This process ensures your mulch beds become and stay weed-free throughout the season.

**Key Features:**
- Root-level weed removal
- Preventative applications (twice yearly)
- Season-long weed-free beds
- Professional maintenance

---

### 7. Landscape Lighting
No more easily broken, cheap plastic solar lights that burn out. Have your property hardwired with a wide variety of fixtures and options for control to take entertaining to the next level.

**Key Features:**
- Hardwired installation
- Durable professional fixtures
- Multiple control options
- Enhanced curb appeal
- Perfect for entertaining

---

### 8. Special Jobs
We offer a variety of additional jobs starting from simple changes to your yard all the way to complete refurbishments.

**Key Features:**
- Custom project work
- Simple yard changes
- Complete landscape refurbishments
- Flexible scope
